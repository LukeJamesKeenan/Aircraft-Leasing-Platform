The problem

Aircraft leasing runs on fragmented tools

Lease data lives in Excel, maintenance records in another system, credit information somewhere else. Nobody has a single source of truth.

⚡

Remarketing is reactive

Teams find out a lease is expiring months before it does, scramble to remarket, and negotiate from a weak position.

📊

Comparable data is guarded

LRF and rent benchmarks are shared informally between people who know each other. There is no structured, queryable database.

🔧

Redelivery is a nightmare

Maintenance reserve tracking, condition disputes, and compensation calculations are managed in massive Excel models that break.

The Platform

Everything in one place

Six Integrated modules, one shared data layer. Every action in one module instantly updates the rest.

Module 01

Portfolio Timeline

A live visual timeline of every lease event, maintenance check, and cash flow across your entire fleet.

  • Analyst action panel with daily priorities
  • Risk flagging across the fleet
  • Horizon filtering from 30 to 180 days
Module 02

Pricing Calculator

Age-adjusted residual value model with PMT-based LRF calculations and XIRR-based IRR output.

  • Supports all major narrowbody and regional types
  • Automatic transaction logging on deal confirmation
  • Funding cost and return spread inputs
Module 03

Credit Risk

Structured lessee creditworthiness scoring across five weighted categories - financial health, operational scale, country risk, airline type, and recent performance.

  • Weighted scoring model with analyst summary
  • Low / Medium / High risk ratings
  • One-click access from Remarketing Pipeline
Module 04

Remarketing Pipeline

Proactive lease expiry tracking with risk scoring, status management, and recommended actions per aircraft.

  • Remarketing Risk Score per lease
  • Urgent / Watch / On Track classification
  • Direct lessee credit risk access
Module 05

Transaction Database

A structured database of completed lease transactions with a built-in Comparables Engine that benchmarks proposed deals against the market.

  • Comparables scored by type, age, region, tenor
  • Market verdict: Strong / At Market / Weak
  • Average LRF benchmarks by aircraft type
Module 06

Redelivery Manager

End-to-end redelivery tracking with maintenance reserve ledger, compensation calculator, and structured checklists.

  • MR balance vs estimated cost with live surplus/deficit
  • Technical, documentation and commercial checklists
  • Compensation item tracking per aircraft
